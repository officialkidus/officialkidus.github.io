About Me

KIDUS DAWIT

At a height of 6 feet 2 inches, with deep black skin and eyes of striking black and brown, I stand as a testament to my Ethiopian heritage. My professionalism, natural talent, and versatility create a dynamic impact on every project. Uniquely equipped to embody the essence of Ambassador Suits, I bring an invigorating perspective to the fashion world.

My name is kidus dawit

Kidus Dawit, an Ethiopian model driven by an unyielding passion for fashion, is highly regarded for his commitment and unparalleled punctuality. Regardless of the situation, he maintains a perfect record of always showing up on time, ensuring he's never late. His dedication has earned the recognition of respected individuals in the industry, including Mr. Bekalu, who once commended him saying, "Your observation is very nice. and you are such a powerfully talented individual in the fashion modeling realm" When Kidus hits the runway for Ambassador Suits, every step he takes reflects his unique style and inherent grace. His keen observation, unwavering dedication, and inherent talent together not only set an inspiring benchmark in the fashion world but also significantly contribute to his ongoing successful professional journey.
